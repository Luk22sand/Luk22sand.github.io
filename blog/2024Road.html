New Year Resolutions for 2024

In 2024, my primary focus is delving into the realm of Artificial Intelligence (AI) with a fervent determination to broaden my understanding of this transformative field. In order to do that I prepapared plan of mesaurable goals that will pave the way for a comprehensive grasp of AI concepts and applications.

Plan for getting more knowledge of Ai:

1. Mastering Python:

    a: Exploring Flask to get knowledge of API.

    b: Exploring FastApi for building fast and modern web APIs.

2. Delving into Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP):

    a: Understanding the significance of NLP in various applications.

    b: Mastering one hot encoding, bag of words, TFIDF, Word2Vec, and AvgWord2Vec.

3. Understanding Basic Deep Learning Concepts:

    a: Diving into the workings of Artificial Neural Networks (ANN).

    b: Understanding the intricacies of multi-layered neural networks.

    c: Grasping forward and backward propagation.

    d: Exploring activation functions, loss functions, and optimizers.

4. Exploring Advanced NLP Concepts:

    a: Exploring projects using OpenAI and Langchain.

    b: Testing Google Gemini to test its capabilities

5. Better understanding of Generative AI:

    a: Diving into advanced NLP techniques like RNN, LSTM RNN, GRU RNN.

    b: Learning about Bidirectional LSTM RNN, Encoder-Decoder, Attention mechanisms, Seq to Seq, and Transformers.

6. Understanding Vector Databases and Stores:

    a: Familiarizing myself with vector databases like ChromaDB and FAISS.

    b: Exploring FAISS vector database utilizing the Facebook AI Similarity Search library.

    b: Getting hands-on experience with Cosmos DB.

7. Deployment of Language Model (LLM) Projects:

    a: Exploring deployment options on AWS and Azure for my language model projects.

    b: Experimenting with LangSmith for efficient model deployment.

8. Building a Private Home AI (Locally Hosted in LAN):

    a: Developing and implementing a locally hosted AI system within my home network.

    b: Incorporating security measures for privacy and data protection.

These resolutions are designed to enhance my skills in Python, machine learning, deep learning, NLP, generative AI, vector databases, and deployment strategies. The inclusion of a private home AI project adds a practical and personalized touch to my learning journey.
