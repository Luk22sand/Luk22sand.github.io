Directional antenna set up

Directional antenna project is the opener to SDR (software defined radio) projects in future – the reason to launch this is to understand radio waves and the way of “sniffing” on them.
Mainly directional antenna narrows the “wave stream” giving option to “point” to WiFi AP or any device broadcasting packets.

Very helpful in this matter is WireShark giving “peaks” on chart whenever antenna points directly to Access Point – which can be used by hacker (once connected to WiFi network) to “knock the door” of target AP that is being under attack.
