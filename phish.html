Phishing tool -- pypfhisher

Phishing these days is very sophisticated way of gathering users’ credentials. Malicious actors are well armed to trick not vigilant user. One of the out of the box tool used to that kind of attack is “pyphisher” written in python. Tool is able to mimic most common login pages to well known portals starting from social media ending on e-mail service providers.
Using proper template and social engineering hacker can successfully deploy “a hook with bait” to target user knowing that people are massively spammed by new Tiktok videos, LinkedIn offers and many other digital messages. Using short urls it’s even harder for the victim to spot weird looking web address especially on phone screem and being in rush of daily WhatsApp, Messenger and Telegram replies just clicks whats coming.
