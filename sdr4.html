SDR - Sun-blinds

*** DISCLAIMER: Sun-blind system was tested with presence of its owner ***

In order to steal the car fob signal a radio jammer device might be very helpful. It gives an option to trick the owner to push key fob min. twice which may produce (depends on car system) 2 rolling codes – “open car” signal.
In other case if the owner is just pressing key fob as muscle memory, not thinking (some of them does that) whether car really closed, it gives a chance using jammer that car will be still opened for some time ..
